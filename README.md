## Pythagorean triples

Generate triples (a, b, c) such that a, b, c are positive integers
and a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup> = c<sup>2</sup>.

```
$ python3 ./pythagoras.py
   a    b    c
==== ==== ====
   3    4    5
   5   12   13
   8   15   17
   7   24   25
  20   21   29
  12   35   37
   9   40   41
  28   45   53
  11   60   61
```
